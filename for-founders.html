As a founder, you make decisions quickly and carry the weight of building something from zero. From day one, we help you set the right foundations, attract talent, close clients, raise capital, and scale globally.

What We Help You Achieve

Just a glimpse of how legal pulls its weight when scaling fast and across borders — pick the ones that matter.

  • Choose the right legal structure and form your company correctly

  • Raise funding and negotiate fair, predictable terms

  • Sign clients, partners, and suppliers with well-built, balanced contracts

  • Hire and incentivise the right team (employment, contractors, ESOPs)

  • Protect your IP, technology, and product

  • Expand into new markets and work with foreign partners

  • Prepare for exits, acquisitions, reorganisations and strategic transactions
